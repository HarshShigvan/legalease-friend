

# LegalEase AI — Landing Page

A beautiful, friendly landing page for LegalEase AI that makes users feel safe and empowered when dealing with legal documents.

## Page Sections

1. **Hero Section** — Big headline with the relatable hook ("Ever signed a lease feeling like you're in a foreign language class?"), a subtitle, and a CTA button. Warm illustration-style background with soft gradients.

2. **Features Section** — 3-4 cards with icons showing core benefits: plain-English summaries, sneaky clause detection, chat-style Q&A, and privacy-first design. Conversational "you" language throughout.

3. **Why It's Lovable Section** — Three pillars highlighted with emoji accents: Privacy 🔒, Ease 💬, Magic ✨. Short, punchy descriptions that feel like a friend talking.

4. **How It Works** — Simple 3-step visual: Upload → Chat → Understand. Clean, approachable design.

5. **Call to Action** — Warm, inviting closing nudge with a prominent button ("Try LegalEase AI — It's Free").

## Design Direction
- Soft, warm color palette (calming blues/greens with friendly accents)
- Rounded corners, generous whitespace
- Conversational typography — friendly sans-serif
- Subtle animations on scroll for delight
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
